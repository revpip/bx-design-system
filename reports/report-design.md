# Report Design Standards

Business X-Ray reports should feel like board-level advisory documents, not plugin exports.

## Report Tone

- Clear
- Practical
- Evidence-led
- Calm
- Action-focused

## Standard Report Sections

1. Cover
2. Executive summary
3. Business Intelligence Score
4. Key strengths
5. Key risks
6. Opportunity map
7. Priority recommendations
8. 30-day sprint
9. 90-day roadmap
10. Appendix

## Visual Rules

- Use generous spacing.
- Avoid dense tables unless essential.
- Make scores visually clear.
- Use recommendation cards for action items.
- Distinguish findings from recommendations.
- Every section should answer "so what?".

## Language Rules

Do not write:

> Your business is failing at marketing.

Write:

> Marketing activity is not yet supported by a consistent measurement system, making it harder to know which activity is creating value.
