# Motion Principles

Motion should explain, reassure or guide. It should never distract.

## Approved Motion Uses

- X-Ray scan sweep on hero sections.
- Business health gauges filling.
- Numbers counting up.
- Timeline progress revealing.
- Cards entering gently as content becomes relevant.

## Rules

- Avoid constant movement in dashboards.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences.
- Keep animations short and subtle.
- Never use motion as the only way to communicate information.

## Brand Motion

The signature Business X-Ray motion is a calm scan line moving across a business surface, revealing hidden opportunities.
