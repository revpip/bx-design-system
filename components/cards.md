# Card Components

Cards are the core container used across Business X-Ray dashboards, reports and landing pages.

## Card Types

### Insight Card

Used for short findings, statistics and recommendations.

Must include:

- title
- supporting text
- optional score/status
- optional action

### Opportunity Card

Used to describe a business opportunity.

Must include:

- opportunity title
- why it matters
- likely benefit
- effort level
- suggested timeframe

### Risk Card

Used for concerns needing attention.

Must include:

- risk title
- severity
- evidence
- recommended next step

### Report Card

Used in report previews and client portal.

Must include:

- report name
- date
- status
- primary action

## Visual Rules

- Rounded corners: 18–28px depending on context.
- Border: subtle, low-opacity neutral.
- Background: `ink` or slightly lighter surface.
- Avoid heavy shadows except on marketing pages.
- Cards should never contain too many competing actions.

## Copy Rules

Card titles should be specific.

Bad:

> Improve systems

Better:

> Quotation process relies too heavily on the owner
